I once used Goodreads, but I find this simple interface much more efficient. I finish maybe one in every ten books that I start, which turns Goodreads into this energy drain that takes all the fun out of reading. Imagine the horror of seeing 43 books in the currently-reading section. Plus, goodreads is one terrible web experience, and HTML is simply elegant. More importantly it is customizable.
